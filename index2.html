Introducing Dantrindo - The Furniture Manufacturer From Indonesia
Contemporary Outdoor Furniture Specialist

DANTRINDO is an Indonesian based factory specialised in modern contemporary outdoor furniture and accessories. The
factory was established by the experts and veterans in the European furniture industry in Sidoarjo (Surabaya), East Java -
Indonesia, where the most qualified skilled worker in the country can be found. DANTRINDO has the production capability to
produce and supply from small to large scale project covering overseas market nearby or around the world. With decades of
combined experience and unique knowledge from our founders, here are some reasons that are your best and most strategic
partner for the long run.

3D Scan & 3D Design


The use of 3D scan technology makes it very easy for us to capture the product mockup desired by the customer, then make improvements and enhancements to the 3D design.

Pipe Laser Cuting Machine


With the pipe laser cutting machine, mass production work become very efficient, and further increases the precision of each product
